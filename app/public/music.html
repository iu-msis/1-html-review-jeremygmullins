Favorite Artist: John Mayer


I have always loved John Mayer's music. He has consistently put out great music for nearly 20 years. I've had the pleasure of seeing John Mayer live 4 times. Each better than the last.


My favorite album from John Mayer is Born and Raised and my favorite song from that record is the title track, "Born and Raised". While this definitely is not Mayer's most popular work, I think it is his most honest. He wrote this record while living in Montana. The record is largely about reflecting on his life and how he got to where here is.
